Privacy

Information on how we process personal data when you use this website or contact us by email. The German privacy notice is legally authoritative for our business in Germany.

Privacy notice

Controller

The controller under the GDPR is Segel Immoverwaltung, represented by Stefan Kosker, Eggensteiner Straße 57, 76297 Stutensee, Germany. Phone: 07244 558 05 02, email: kontakt@segel-immo.de

Hosting

The site is delivered via an external hosting service. When you access pages, the provider processes technically necessary data (such as IP address, time of access and related technical metadata) to serve content and to keep the service secure. The legal basis is Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in secure, stable operation). Retention follows the hosting provider’s technical configuration. When you have the provider’s legal name and privacy documentation, add a link or name here for full transparency.

Email contact

If you email us, we process the data you send (sender, content and related metadata) to handle your request. The legal basis is Art. 6 (1) lit. b GDPR for service-related enquiries, otherwise Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR. Data is deleted once the request is completed, unless statutory retention obligations apply.

Cookies and analytics

This site does not use cookies for advertising or analytics and, at the time of writing, does not embed third-party analytics or marketing tools.

Your rights

You have the rights under the GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability and objection where the conditions are met, as well as the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Updates

We update this notice when our website or processing changes. Please review it from time to time.

Contact

Whether you want to set up management afresh, prepare a handover or simply see whether we are a good fit — please call or email. We listen to your situation, give a first assessment and say openly what the sensible next step is. We usually respond within one business day. A few brief details about the property, location and current topic help us prepare well.
